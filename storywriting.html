Writing a Story

Indoor/Outdoor

Writing stories can be great fun! You could do it on a rainy day! All you need to do is ask yourself some questions and then you can start writing!


STEP 1: Decide on a main character and describe where the story will be set!


STEP 2: What will your character do in the story?


STEP 3: What problem will occur?


STEP 4: How will the problem be solved?


Now that you know these questions, write your story! Make sure you use lots of describing words!
