How to compose classical music

indoor/outdoor/online

Here's my guide on how to compose great classical musics:

  1. Get some pencils, pen, paper or a music composing software.
  2. Get some inspiration, try listening to other composer's music or try to make the music to picture a scene.
  3. If you're stressed take a break.
  4. Great! now make more compositions!
